# User Interviews

## Interview 1: David, CTO at Seed Stage Startup (8 employees)
**Date:** 2026-05-10
**Key Findings:**
- They were paying for 8 Claude Pro seats but also had a $50/mo API bill for internal tools.
- David was surprised by the "Retail vs Credits" rule; he didn't realize the overlap was so direct.
- He loved the benchmark bar: "I always wonder if I'm overspending compared to other YC companies."

## Interview 2: Sarah, Engineering Manager at Series A Startup (45 employees)
**Date:** 2026-05-11
**Key Findings:**
- "We have a lot of 'zombie' seats for ChatGPT Team."
- The "Engineer Count vs Team Size" validation in the form made her realize they were counting non-engineers in their developer tool budget.
- Requested a "PDF Export" of the benchmark to show her CFO.

## Summary of Insights
1. **Benchmark Confidence:** Users want to know they are "normal" compared to peers.
2. **Subscription Fatigue:** Teams often lose track of where they are paying for the same model (Web UI vs API).
3. **Admin Controls:** Larger teams (10+) value the administrative consolidation more than the raw cost savings, but the cost savings is what gets them to use the tool.
